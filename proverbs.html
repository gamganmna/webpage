Vietnamese Proverbs

Examples of Vietnamese proverbs and their meanings. What do you think about them?

"Ăn cỏ non, uống nước suối ăn no mà không biết mình đói"

Translation: "Eating tender grass and drinking pure water, not knowing you’re hungry"

To be unaware of one’s own needs or situation. This proverb is used when someone is too absorbed in their own activities or problems to assess their own situation.

"Ăn no mới biết đói"

Translation: "Only know you’re hungry after eating"

This proverb means to only understand the importance of something once you have experienced it yourself.

"Bầu ơi thương lấy bí cò"

Meaning: "Be careful with what you say; it may come back to haunt you in the future."

This proverb warns against saying things one may regret later.

"Bắt đầu từ đầu"

Meaning: "To take a new approach to achieve success; starting from the beginning"

This proverb is used when someone needs to start over after trying unsuccessfully to achieve something.
