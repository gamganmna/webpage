Explore unique Vietnamese words and expressions that offer insight into the culture, values, and everyday life of Vietnam.

Người yêu

This term is used to describe a loved one or significant other, capturing a deep sense of affection in Vietnamese relationships.

Trời ơi

A popular expression that conveys surprise, frustration, or disbelief. It is often used to react to unexpected situations.

Duyên

This term speaks to the concept of fate or destiny, especially when it brings people together in unique and meaningful ways.
